Overview

Purpose

Ciudad del Carmen Chamber of Commerce is a leader of change that represents, promotes and manages with social responsibility, the construction of agreements and alliances between the productive, educational and government sectors, to strengthen the comprehensive development of companies and society.

Scenarios

  • What are the upcoming events in the area?
  • What groups or organizations can I join?
  • What permits do I need to open a business?
